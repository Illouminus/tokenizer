# Ebaillot42 (EB42) Whitepaper

This token is created as part of the School 42 Tokenizer project. 

**Purpose:** EB42 is a test token designed to demonstrate the core functionalities of the BEP-20 standard on the BNB Smart Chain.

**Features:**
- **Transfers:** Users can send and receive EB42 tokens seamlessly.
- **Burnable:** Token holders can destroy their own tokens to reduce the total supply.
- **Pausable:** The contract owner holds the privilege to pause all token transfers in case of an emergency or a security breach, protecting the community.

---

### How to Setup and Use EB42

To interact with the EB42 token, you need to configure your Web3 wallet (e.g., MetaMask) to connect to the BNB Smart Chain Testnet.

**Step 1: Add the BNB Smart Chain Testnet to MetaMask**
1. Open MetaMask, click on the network dropdown at the top left, and select **"Add network"**.
2. Scroll down and click **"Add a network manually"**.
3. Fill in the following details:
   - **Network Name:** BNB Smart Chain Testnet
   - **New RPC URL:** `https://data-seed-prebsc-1-s1.bnbchain.org:8545` *(or `https://rpc.ankr.com/bsc_testnet_chapel`)*
   - **Chain ID:** `97`
   - **Currency Symbol:** `tBNB`
   - **Block Explorer URL:** `https://testnet.bscscan.com`
4. Click **"Save"** and switch to the newly added network.

**Step 2: Import the EB42 Token**
1. In MetaMask, go to the **"Tokens"** tab and click **"Import tokens"**.
2. Paste the EB42 Contract Address: `0x076abcc0d679529d24f08b65a26f5d4bca8c904d`
3. The Token Symbol (EB42) and Decimals (18) should autofill.
4. Click **"Next"** and **"Import"**.

You are now ready to send, receive, and view your EB42 balance! Note: You will need a small amount of tBNB for gas fees to execute transactions.
